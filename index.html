I am a PhD student at the Observatoire astronomique de Strasbourg, France. I am working under the supervision of Dominique Aubert (Prof. at the University of Strasbourg). My topic of interest is the study of the Epoch of Reionisation (EoR) through the numerical simulations (cosmological simulations as EMMA or semi-analytical ones as 21cmFAST) that I explore through a its topology.
