Emilie Thélie

Postdoctoral Fellow  •  University of Texas at Austin  •  Cosmology  •  Epoch of Reionisation

I am a postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Texas at Austin working with Julian Munoz (Prof. at the UT Austin). My goal is to improve the understanding of the Epoch of Reionisation by developing theoretical tools and working on the analysis of the upcoming 21 cm that will be produced by radio-telescopes like the Hydrogren Epoch of Reionisation Array or the Square Kilometer Array.

BLAAAAA I got my PhD in 2023, for which I worked at the Observatoire astronomique de Strasbourg (ObAS), France, under the supervision of Dominique Aubert (Prof. at the University of Strasbourg). I mainly studied the Epoch of Reionisation (EoR) with numerical simulations (cosmological simulations as EMMA or semi-analytical ones as 21cmFAST) that I explored through the topology of its evolution.
