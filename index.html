Emilie Thélie

Postdoctoral Fellow  •  University of Texas at Austin  •  Cosmology  •  Epoch of Reionisation

I am a PhD student at the Observatoire astronomique de Strasbourg, France. I am working under the supervision of Dominique Aubert (Prof. at the University of Strasbourg). I mainly study the Epoch of Reionisation (EoR) with numerical simulations (cosmological simulations as EMMA or semi-analytical ones as 21cmFAST) that I explore through the topology of its evolution. My work takes place in the context of the upcoming 21 cm that will be produced by radio-telescopes like the Square Kilometer Array.
